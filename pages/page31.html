DEFINITION

Disbelief that your product can provide the stated benefit. It occurs after you reinforce the suspected benefit.

HOW TO DEAL WITH SKEPTICISM

Restate what you have just said using strong and confident words.

  1. Voice tone provides you with 50% of success.
  2. Offer documents such as trials, journals....etc.
  3. Give it to the customer and ask for commitment and action. Set a date in which you will deliver the document and Stick accurately to it.
  4. Provide the requested document and highlight the area of the customer interest with light marker.
  5. Get the customer agreement and then ask for action.
