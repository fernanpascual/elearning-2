Winning Over Difficult Customers :
Listen Actively

  • Step 3: Listen Actively
  • Resist the temptation to ask questions
  • Nod your head or use a verbal nod of the head –
  • “Uh-huh”
  • “Go on”
  • “I see”
  • “I understand"
    •
  • Listen carefully for the central theme of the person’s problem or compliant
  • Take notes and be prepared to restate what you’ve heard.
