DEFINITION

A legitimate shortcoming or disadvantage of your product. It is the most serious attitude, and handling it inefficiently may spoil the meeting

HOW TO DEAL WITH REAL OBJECTION

  • Acknowledge the customer opinion, since you can not deny it.
  • Use the YES...BUT technique.
  • Minimize the customer objection by maximizing the other great benefits of your product.
  • Try to position your product in an indication in which its disadvantage is not of great importance.
