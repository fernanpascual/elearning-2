Sales Objection 3:
“I’m not interested”

  • Customer : I am not interested
  • Salesperson : Oh, really? Why not?
  • Customer : I have looked at similar things already and think it does not suit my business
  • Salesperson : Do you recall why was it not suitable for your business Sir?

  • This is the standard brush off statement that customers use. It will take you multiple questions to get to the bottom of his concern. Once you know it, then you can win your sale.




Your best response is
“Think it Over”
