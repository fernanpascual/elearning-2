Customer Resistance

Type
Definition
Example
Misconception
An incorrect negative assumption about your product, due to a lack of information or misinformation.
“This does not offer anything more the than my current advertising“
Real objection
A legitimate shortcoming or disadvantage of your product.
“The price is too expensive for me.”
Lack of Interest
Disinterest in your product because of satisfaction with a competitor product.
“I am very satisfied with Y”
Skepticism
Disbelief that your product can provide the stated benefit.
“I find it hard to believe that X has such a prolonged effect.”
