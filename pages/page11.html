Sales Objection 2:
“I will have to take a look at it and then confirm”

  • Customer : I will have to take a look at it and then confirm

  • Salesperson : Definitely. This is a business decision. I can help you evaluate the same and then you can decide on the proposal.


  • Offer to help , before the customer can say Yes or No.

  • This adds a limit on how long the customer will think about it and helps to prevent from forgetting about it altogether
