Calming Irate customers

  • Customers do not start out irate
    • They’re typically just frustrated or confused
  • In most cases, anger can be avoided
    • Use the technique for handling difficult situations
    • Handle “moments of truth” properly
  • Customers experience varying degrees of anger
  • Initially, the customer describes the inconvenience of a problem
  • To calm a customer and gain his or her confidence
    • Acknowledge the customers frustration
    • Offer help
